The Outback Advantage

The Outback Series Canopy Camper is not your average bolt-on accessory. It’s a product born from the demands of hardcore overlanders and refined with precision Australian engineering.

Here’s why this canopy camper is a game-changer:

  • Revolutionary One-Piece Roof Design
    Camp King’s exclusive one-piece tent roof is fully TIG welded and reinforced with a specialized Orlock riveting system. This makes it one of the most structurally solid campers on the market, able to withstand the harshest conditions while providing a safe, comfortable shelter.
  • Maximized Storage Without Sacrificing Bed Utility
    Unlike many campers that require rear fit kits or awkward adapters, this camper allows you to retain your factory tailgate. You’ll still have access to your tailgate workspace while seamlessly integrating drawers, fridge slides, or even a full slide-out kitchen system.
  • Tailored Fit – No Dead Space
    Every Camp King Outback Canopy Camper is designed specifically for your truck model, no universal fit kits, no wasted space. This ensures tight tolerances, clean aesthetics, and efficient use of every inch of your Tacoma’s bed.
  • Built for Overlanders, by Overlanders
    Designed in Australia and manufactured at Camp King’s Brisbane factory, these campers reflect the highest levels of craftsmanship with no cheap outsourced labor. This is gear made by people who live and breathe overlanding.

Key Features and Benefits That Fire Us Up

Let’s get into the real reasons why we’re so fired up about the Outback Series Canopy Camper:

1. Unmatched Build Quality

  • 100% Australian-made at Camp King’s factory in Brisbane, QLD.
  • Laser-cut 3mm TIG welded aluminum body and 2mm aluminum top for unbeatable strength and lightweight performance.
  • Superior powder coat finish resists sun fade and harsh conditions.

2. Lightweight Yet Rugged

  • Weighing only 340 lbs (147 kg), it combines a canopy and rooftop tent into one streamlined unit. This keeps your rig’s weight low while maintaining structural toughness.

3. One-Piece Tent Roof Design

  • TIG welded with Orlock riveting for superior strength and durability.
  • Eliminates weak points found in multi-piece designs.

4. Modular Interior Access & Sleeping Platform

  • The two-piece sleeping platform measures 85" (L) x 51.2" (W), perfect for tall folks.
  • The rear section pivots up for easy access to your truck bed from inside the tent.
  • Maximum rear opening height is over 5.5ft, allowing you to stand up and move comfortably inside.

5. Premium Comfort Features

  • New high-quality 4-inch high-density two-piece mattress included.
  • Tent prewired with lighting and power ports as standard.
  • 10mm closed-cell foam insulation on the internal roof regulates temperature in extreme climates.

6. All-Weather Protection

  • Full tent body constructed from military-grade Wax Convertor Coolabah Tear Stop Canvas (AS2001.2.18.1987).
  • Heavy-duty 3M industrial-grade seals on all openings keep dust and water out, even in extreme conditions.
  • Large canvas front awning structurally designed to withstand high winds no extra poles needed.

7. Optimized Storage & Usability

  • Retain your truck's factory tailgate for added workspace.
  • Compatible with slide-out fridges, kitchen slides, drawers, and Goose Gear’s Full Interior Camp System.
  • Six internal storage pouches included.
  • Stainless steel latches with padlock points for maximum security.

  • Internal mount for LED light bar.

8. Superior Ventilation & Visibility

  • Two large insect-proof mesh side windows (59" W x 34.4" H).
  • One large front door with a full-length built-in window (60.6" H x 30.7" W).
  • Included bungee cords ensure the canvas folds neatly when closing the tent.
